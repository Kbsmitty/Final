Our Story.

Outdoor Adventures, our family owned company based out of Bozeman, Montana has always been focused on outdoor recreation, which includes getting others out!

In 1964, the Robertsons became proud owners of the Double 7 Ranch near Bozeman.

This would eventually lead to ownership and the creation of Outdoor Adventures. Our passion is to transport others to a variety of locations in Montana, doing what we love, has always been something we wanted to share with others. From our rich history within the Gallatin Valley, we have always been interested in showing others the beauty of Bozeman and the surrounding area.

Bozeman’s unique location offers access to some of the most spectacular landscapes in North America. From the rugged peaks of the Bridger Range to the expansive waters of the Gallatin River, our tours encompass a variety of outdoor activities including hiking, mountain biking, fly fishing, rock climbing, and winter sports. We are also proud to offer specialized tours that can include photography workshops, wildlife observation, and historical explorations of the rich heritage of the area.
